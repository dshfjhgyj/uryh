Working As A House Cleaner: A Day In The Life

So what would your day look like? Fast, furious, and fun is a good way to describe it.

All the equipment and cleaning solutions you’ll need for your cleaning jobs will be provided to you once you complete training, and you will keep them with you at all times, refilling supplies at your leisure as needed from our “Refill Station”. Each day you will typically drive to 1 to 3 jobs, depending on size and scope of work, cleaning somewhere between 6-8 labor hours. It’s important to be on time for your first job of the day, in case customers have any questions or concerns, and to make sure that you have the time you need for your bookings.

If you only have one job, that means it is a deep clean and you will clean it from top to bottom — literally. We give all of our clients a comprehensive initial clean so be ready to work and scrub if needed. You’re more than welcome to listen to music, podcasts, or audiobooks while you clean if that makes you more productive.

You’ll repeat these steps throughout the day if you have more than one job. (See why we need morning people?) At the end of the day, which usually is around 5 pm, you’ll pack up your equipment and supplies, log your miles, and go home for the night.

And remember, no nights or weekends are ever expected! You deserve time off to refocus and prepare for another day of impacting the lives of homeowners in Central Indiana.
